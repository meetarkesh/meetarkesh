I'm an **AI Engineer** focused on building intelligent software products that combine modern AI with exceptional user experiences.

My work sits at the intersection of **LLMs, AI Agents, automation systems, product engineering, and scalable software architecture.** I enjoy transforming complex workflows into elegant consumer products that feel effortless to use.
