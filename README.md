# About Me

I'm an **AI Engineer and Product Builder** passionate about building intelligent software that solves real-world problems through exceptional user experiences.

My work sits at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence**, **Software Engineering**, and **Consumer Product Development**—transforming ideas into scalable applications powered by modern AI systems.

I enjoy designing products from first principles, architecting full-stack applications, integrating LLMs into production workflows, and building automation systems that create measurable business impact.

Previously, I worked on **multimodal AI research at Samsung Research (PRISM)**, developing deep learning models for speaker diarization and audio-visual intelligence. I also build AI-powered automation systems for businesses, focusing on workflow optimization, conversational AI, and intelligent decision-making.

My long-term goal is to build category-defining AI products used by millions of people.
