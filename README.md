# About Me

I am an **AI Engineer** passionate about designing intelligent products that combine **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Full-Stack Engineering, and modern software architecture** to solve real-world problems.

My work sits at the intersection of **AI systems engineering**, **product development**, and **automation**, with a focus on building scalable applications powered by **Large Language Models, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, and cloud-native infrastructure**.

I enjoy transforming ideas into production-ready software—from architecting backend systems and designing intuitive user experiences to integrating advanced AI capabilities that deliver measurable business impact.

My engineering philosophy is centered around:

- Designing scalable, maintainable software systems
- Building AI products that solve meaningful business problems
- Writing clean, production-quality code
- Shipping products with an engineering-first mindset
- Continuously learning emerging technologies in AI and Software Engineering

Over the past few years, I have worked across **AI automation**, **multimodal machine learning research**, **product engineering**, and **full-stack application development**, contributing to projects ranging from enterprise automation workflows to deep learning research in speaker diarization and intelligent document processing.
